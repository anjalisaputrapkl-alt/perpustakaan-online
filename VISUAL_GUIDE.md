## Interaction States

### Button States

**Pinjam Button:**
- Default: Blue background (#0B3D61)
- Hover: Darker blue with shadow
- Active: Pressed effect with darker shade
- Disabled: Gray background

**Detail Button:**
- Default: Outline style (border only)
- Hover: Filled with light accent color
- Active: Filled with darker accent color

### Card States

- Default: Flat appearance
- Hover: Elevated with shadow & border color change
- Focus: Outline for keyboard navigation
